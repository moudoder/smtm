About Us

MTM HOLDING was founded in 2014 and is a group of companies working in the field of modern financial technologies. Over seven years of work considerable experience has been gained, a number of investment and technological projects have been implemented, and a proprietary digital information processing and storage center has been created and certified according to the highest level of Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS).

When building the holding, we focused on the growing needs of customers in the field of e-commerce and the crypto industry, in parallel developing co-profile areas.

Currently , the holding includes the following companies:

SMTM HOLDING OU

Carries out strategic management of the group's companies, plans and organizes the development of new directions, supports the work of the information processing and storage Center.

ExFrame OU (Licensed as a cryptocurrency provider - MTR Estonia)

Provides services for the purchase and sale of cryptocurrencies to a wide range of legal entities and individuals, maintains mobile wallets of clients, and is engaged in cryptoprocessing. The company has a well-developed network of branches and representative offices, connected to the world's largest cryptocurrency exchanges.

- Magua Pay INC (Licensed by the standards of Money Service Business - Canada)

Specializes in providing processing and acquiring services, also actively works with cryptocurrencies.

TomPay Ltd (Licensed by the standards of Electronic Money Institution - UK )

Is engaged in opening accounts (IBAN) for legal entities and individuals, in the near future plans to obtain direct membership with Master Card, with the subsequent implementation of a program of issuing payment cards for various enterprises of the group and its retail customers.

According to the results of 2019 – 2020, SMTM HOLDING was awarded the STRONGEST IN ESTONIA 2020 rating.
